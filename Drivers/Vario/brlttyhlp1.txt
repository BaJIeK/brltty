---BRLTTY Baum Vario 40 Help Screen---

T[LR]n are the top-keys.
CR(n) means cursor routing keys.
"+" means keys are combined.

Movement:
  One line up/down: TL1 / TL3
  Window left/right: TL2/TR2
  Goto cursor: TL1+TL3
  Cursor tracking: TL2+TL3+TR1+TR2
  Goto top left of screen: TL1+TL2
  Goto bottom left of screen: TL2+TL3
  One character left/right: CR1+CR2 / CR39+CR40

Simulation of keyboard arrow keys:
  up/down arrow: TR1/TR3

Special modes
  This help: TL1+TL2+TR2 (toggle)
  Freeze/unfreeze screen: TL1+TL2+TR1
  Attribute underlining on/off:
       TL1+TL3+TR3
  Show attribute representations:
       TL1+TL2+TL3+TR3
  Info mode: TL2+TR1

Cut & paste:
  Mark beginning of block: CR39+CR40+CRn
  Mark end of block: CR1+CR2+CRn
  Mark from m to n: CRm+CR(m+2)+CRn
  Paste cut region: CR2+CR3

Preferences:
  Preferences menu:
    Enter/exit preferences menu: TL1+TR1
      In the menu:
      Previous/next setting option
       for this item: TR1/TR3
      Previous/next menu item: TL1/TL3
-----End of help screen-----
