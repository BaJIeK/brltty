Help for SyncBraille

lu/ld: go up/down one line
ru/rd: go left/right one window

lu+ld: go to beginning of current line
lu+ld + ru/rd: go to beginning of top/bottom line

RU+RD: go to cursor or undo last cursor tracking motion
ru+rd+lu: switch to/from status line
ru+rd+ld: switch to/from preferences menu

ru+lu: toggle cursor tracking on/off
ru+ld: toggle 6/8-dot braille

rd+lu: freeze/unfreeze screen image
rd+ld: toggle attributes/text display

lu+ld+ru+rd: switch to/from help page
